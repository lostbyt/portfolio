Tasty World

What is it?
Tastyworld is a mobile application that allows users to discover the various culinary flavors from around the world in their nearby surroundings, offering them new taste experiences. Tagline: "Worldwide dishes" The user first indicates the type of dishes they are looking for and their budget. The application will then provide them with a selection of addresses that match their craving with distance indication. A direction will be suggested to them based on their choice.

Who's it for?
The "TastyWorld" application is aimed at all food lovers, epicureans, and those curious about food culture.

Where does it live?
The application is available on Android and Apple smartphones. Once the application is downloaded, users simply need to fill out a questionnaire regarding their food preferences (taste, allergy...), which will serve as the starting point for their profile. T his profile will evolve and become more refined over time based on their experiences. A social media sharing feature is a potential option for future development.
