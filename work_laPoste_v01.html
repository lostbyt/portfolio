La Poste

I did this animation for the French postal service "La Poste", it was used during an internal convention to highlight the new service segmentation. The idea behind this animation is based on the need for connectivity and exchange between the various departments of the company.
I was responsible for all aspects of the project (the conception, animation, shading and compositing.) Made with Blender and AfterEffects.
